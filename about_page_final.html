Questions about working with us

Everything You Wanted to Ask
Before You Reach Out

Who is behind Cher Digital Analytics? ✦
Cher Digital is a team of four specialists — a CEO & Founder, Web Developer, Social Media Designer and HR & Board Member — all focused exclusively on marketing for salon and beauty businesses.
Do you only work with salons? ✦
Yes. We work exclusively with hair salons, nail studios, med spas, blow dry bars, lash studios and other beauty businesses. Deep industry focus means better results for our clients.
Will I have a dedicated person on my account? ✦
Yes. Every client has a dedicated team member who knows their goals, their market and their numbers. At end of every month we walk you through your report on a call — personally.
Are there lock-in contracts? ✦
Never. Everything is month-to-month. If it's not working, you cancel — no penalty, no notice period, no awkward invoice for the rest of the year. We believe good work speaks for itself.
