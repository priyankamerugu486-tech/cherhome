Why Cher exists · The origin story

The best salons kept losing
to the loudest ones.

We kept seeing it — incredible stylists, talented nail techs, spa owners who'd built something genuinely special, getting buried on Google by competitors who were just better at marketing. Not better at the actual work. Just louder online.

That felt wrong. So Charishma built Cher to fix it. A team that only works with beauty businesses, that actually learns your market, and that treats your growth like it's personal — because for us, it is.

We only work with beauty
No dentists. No restaurants. No "we do a bit of everything." Just salons, spas, and studios — because deep focus beats broad dabbling every time.
We earn it every month
No contracts that trap you. We do good work, you stay. We don't, you walk. It's the only arrangement that keeps us honest.

Four people. Zero bots. All yours.

These are the actual humans working on your salon's growth — reading your reports, building your website, designing your content, and keeping everything running. No outsourcing. No juniors learning on your dime.
