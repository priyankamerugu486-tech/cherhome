"Can't I just do this myself with Canva and a Squarespace site?
You can get started, yes. But ranking on Google, optimising your Google Business Profile, running ads that convert, AND getting cited on ChatGPT and Perplexity is not a Canva job — it's a full-time job. Most salon owners who try it spend 10+ hours a week and still don't move the needle on bookings.
"I already have a freelancer handling my social. Why do I need Cher?
Social content is one piece. Your freelancer almost certainly isn't touching your Google Business Profile, local SEO rankings, Google Ads, or AI search visibility. If no one is connecting all of those dots, you're leaving most of your bookings on the table.
"Why not just use a local digital agency?
A general agency will try. But they'll apply the same strategy they use for a plumber or a law firm. Salons have a specific booking cycle, client psychology, and local search pattern that generic agencies consistently miss. We don't work with anyone who isn't in the beauty industry.
"What's this AI search thing? My clients just use Google.
For now. But over 400 million people use ChatGPT weekly, and AI Overviews now appear on more than 50% of Google results. The salon owners who rank on AI search now will be nearly impossible to displace in 18 months. Those who wait will be starting from zero.
"We're too small / just one location. Is this overkill?
Our most common client is a single-location salon with 2–5 chairs. You don't need to be big to benefit from being found on Google and AI search. In fact, small salons have the biggest upside — because most of your local competitors still aren't optimised at all.
"What if it doesn't work? I'm locked into a contract.
There are no contracts at Cher. Everything is month-to-month. If it's not working and we can't fix it, you cancel — no penalty, no invoice for the rest of the year. We earn your business every single month. That's the only arrangement that keeps us honest.
