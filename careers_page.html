Everything you wanted to know about working with Cher Digital — answered honestly.

Are the roles fully remote? +
Yes. Both roles are 100% remote and based in India. You'll be working with a US-based team — so strong English communication and the ability to work across time zones is important.
What's the stipend for the Social Media Designer role? +
₹25,000–₹30,000 per month for a 3-month contract, starting March 2026. There is a clear opportunity for full-time conversion based on your performance during the contract.
Is a portfolio really mandatory for the design role? +
Yes — without exception. Applications without a portfolio will not be reviewed. Your portfolio must show social media design samples, short-form video work, and strong brand consistency. A link to your work is fine.
What certifications do I need for the marketing contractor role? +
Google Analytics and Google Ads certifications are required. Please include your certification links or screenshots when you apply. Experience with local businesses or salon brands is a strong advantage.
How quickly will I hear back? +
If shortlisted, you'll hear from us within 48 hours of applying. If you haven't heard back within 5 business days, you're welcome to follow up at hello@cherdigitalanalytics.com.
