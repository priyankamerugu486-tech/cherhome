Questions we get asked

Everything You Wanted to Ask
But Were Too Busy to Google

Why isn't my salon showing up on Google? ✦
Usually it's three things — your Google Business Profile is incomplete, your website doesn't use the right local keywords, or you don't have enough reviews to outrank competitors. We fix all three.
Do I need to know anything about marketing? ✦
Not a single thing. You tell us your goals, we take it from there. Most of our clients have never run a Google Ad in their lives — that's literally why we exist.
How quickly will I see results? ✦
Ads can drive traffic within the first week. SEO takes 60–90 days to build real momentum. Most clients see a meaningful jump in bookings within their first 2–3 months.
Will I actually understand the reports? ✦
Yes — that's the whole point. We write them in plain English and walk you through them on a call every month. If something doesn't make sense, you ask and we explain until it does.
Am I locked into a contract? ✦
Never. Everything is month-to-month. If it's not working, you cancel — no penalty, no 30-day notice, no invoice for the rest of the year. We don't believe in trapping people.
What's the free audit actually like? ✦
A PDF that breaks down your Google presence, local visibility, and website. We show you exactly what's costing you bookings and give you 3 quick wins — whether you work with us or not.
